About the Website

This is MY website.

It is a space for me to showcase my skills as a web developer and designer.

Everything you see and use, or in other words the entire User Experience and User Interface, was designed and coded by me. The ONLY external resources that are utilized are several online libraries for icons, fonts, and free stock images and music. There are no frameworks or libraries for the HTML, CSS, or JavaScript.

All the code I wrote from scratch, but of course I had help along the way in the form of online courses and tutorials.
